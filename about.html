Hi I'm Vanessa.

I do international digital expansion into Latin American markets for mobile and technology startup companies.

I'm fascinated by the role software and mobile technology are taking in our lives, and in the way their adoption is changing cultures all over the world. I focus on the tech sector and its effects on consumer behavior, mostly U.S. Hispanic (in South Florida), Brazil, Colombia, Argentina and now Cuba.

I'm from South Florida, born and raised. I attended the University of Florida during the Muschamp years, where I majored in Public Relations and minored in both French and Business Administration. I studied abroad for a semester in Paris, at La Sorbonne.

I've held various startup jobs, mostly at music tech startups like eMotion Technologies, ToneRite, and Grooveshark (which sadly no longer exists). Most recently, I operated as head of international expansion and artist relations at Setmine. I've held other positions, working with media and tech companies both in the U.S. and abroad. For the past year and a half, I've worked as a strategy consultant for tech startups out of South Florida.

I've lived in Bogotá and Paris. I speak Spanish, French and Portuguese. I'm an avid traveller, and keep that list growing to remain up-to-date with international markets.

I love dogs, wine, being outdoors and salsa dancing. If you'd like to get in touch, email me at vanessa at eternemedia dot com, or you can always tweet at me @vanessais_.
