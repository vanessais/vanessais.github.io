Hi I'm Vanessa.

Welcome to my extensive bio.

Why so extensive? Well, because everything I write is my own opinion, based upon my knowledge and my past experiences. Take my words with a grain of salt, and understand where I'm coming from.

I do international digital expansion into Latin American markets for mobile and technology startup companies.

I'm fascinated by the role mobile technology is taking in our lives, and in the way its adoption is changing cultures all over the world. I focus on U.S. Hispanic (in South Florida), Brazil, Colombia, Argentina and now Cuba.

I'm from South Florida, born and raised. I attended the University of Florida during the Muschamp years, where I majored in Public Relations and minored in both French and Business Administration. I studied abroad for a semester in Paris, at La Sorbonne.

My first startup job was as director of public relations for eMotion Technologies, followed by director of marketing at ToneRite, and then on the international team at Grooveshark (which sadly no longer exists). Most recently, I operated as head of international expansion and artist relations at Setmine. I've held other positions, working with media and tech companies both in the U.S. and abroad.

I played women's rugby in college, but got injured and stopped. Nowadays, I run. I eat lean and do my best to live a healthy lifestyle.

I've lived in Bogotá and Paris. I also speak fluent Spanish, French and Portuguese. I'm an avid traveller, and have been to 13 countries.

I love dogs, wine, being outdoors and salsa dancing. If you'd like to get in touch, email me at vanessa at eternemedia dot com, or you can always tweet at me @vanessais_.
